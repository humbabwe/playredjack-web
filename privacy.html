Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Privacy is the standard. Those who violate people's privacy are evil. Full stop. We don't do it.

What We Collect

Data stored on your device

redjack stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your redjack token balance and weekly reload date
  • Practice statistics (hands played, accuracy, win/loss records)
  • Shoe progress (so you can resume a session)
  • Active module session state

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Purchase data

If you make an in-app purchase (subscription or token pack), your transaction is processed through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect:

  • A randomized device identifier
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • App version and device type

RevenueCat does not collect your name, email address, or payment information. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or usage tracking

Children's Privacy

redjack is not directed at children under 13. We don't collect information about anyone anyway, so if your kids stumble upon us, we won't be collecting theirs either.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
